Key Signatures

This plan can be the basis for an advance directive, but it is not a legal document. You can take the following steps to help insure that your plan will be followed:

  • Make sure your plan is "doable", your supporters can follow the plan and it does not include any unreasonable demands.
  • Include the phone number of your state agency of protection and advocacy. Tell your supporters to contact them if your wishes are not being honored.
  • Make paper copies of this document, have them all signed by at least two trusted supporters and a person who has been given "Power of Attorney", distribute these copies to your supporters and to any agencies or places where you might be taken for protection or to receive treatment.
